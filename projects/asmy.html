Community Marketing

ASMY Mt Gravatt Digital Marketing System

A community marketing system for socials, website updates, event promotion and content creation for a yoga and meditation centre.

Business challenge

The work involved manual processes, multiple platforms and repeatable communication tasks that could be improved through clearer systems and AI-assisted workflows.

Solution

I structured the process into a repeatable business system: capture information, organise tasks, create content or responses, update the website/platform, and follow up with clients or customers.

AI-ready workflow

Input → AI-assisted organisation → website/platform update → customer communication → reporting and follow-up.

ToolsChatGPT, Cloudflare, WordPress, Shopify, Google Workspace, Canva and business platforms.
OutcomeFaster content production, clearer event promotion and improved community engagement workflow.
